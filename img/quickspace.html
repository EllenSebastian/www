Welcome to QuickSpace

QuickSpace is the solution to today’s sharing economy: your location-based, on-demand space rental platform while you are on the go. QuickSpace is your “home” for just an hour or two to take a quick nap, conduct a quick meeting, or teach a yoga class to a group of friends. A private space that you can book while on the go, straight from a mobile app, while roaming around a loud and crowded city. It’s simple, easy, and convenient.
